Step-by-step disaster preparedness and survival protocols

🌍

Earthquake Survival Guide

What to do before, during and after an earthquake to ensure your safety.

  • 1 DROP to the ground immediately
  • 2 COVER under sturdy furniture
  • 3 HOLD ON until shaking stops
  • 4 Move to open area after quake
  • 5 Check for injuries & gas leaks
🌊

Flood Safety Protocol

How to prepare for floods and safely evacuate your family and valuables.

  • 1 Move to higher ground immediately
  • 2 Never walk in moving floodwater
  • 3 Turn off utilities at main switch
  • 4 Pack emergency kit & documents
  • 5 Call NDRF helpline: 1078
🌀

Cyclone Preparedness

Essential steps to prepare for a cyclone and stay safe during the storm.

  • 1 Stay indoors, away from windows
  • 2 Secure all loose outdoor items
  • 3 Prepare 3-day emergency supply
  • 4 Know your evacuation route
  • 5 Cyclone helpline: 1070
🔥

Fire Emergency Guide

How to react quickly and safely when a fire breaks out at home or workplace.

  • 1 Call Fire Brigade: 101 immediately
  • 2 Activate fire alarm & alert others
  • 3 Use stairs — never the elevator
  • 4 Stay low — crawl under smoke
  • 5 STOP, DROP & ROLL if on fire
☀️

Heatwave Safety

Protecting yourself and others during extreme heat conditions.

  • 1 Stay indoors 11am–4pm
  • 2 Drink water every 15-20 mins
  • 3 Wear light, loose cotton clothing
  • 4 Check on elderly neighbors
  • 5 Never leave children in cars
🎒

Emergency Kit Checklist

Essential items every family should have ready before any disaster strikes.

  • 1 Water: 1L per person per day (3 days)
  • 2 Non-perishable food (3-day supply)
  • 3 First aid kit + prescription meds
  • 4 Flashlight, radio, extra batteries
  • 5 Copies of ID, insurance, cash
